About Credibility.news

"read stories from the both sides."

Credibility.news is initiated to contribute a space for netizens to collect and read stories from different perspectives. It aim sto create awareness among users on rethinking/reviewing the credibility of various public articles and publishers.

This is a project specially tribute to @aaronsw, for his contribution, inspiration to the internet and human mankind.

Technology stack:

  • webpy (by @aaronsw)
  • twitter bootstrap
  • sqlachemy
  • github
  • dokku

  • *site layout is inspired by hackernews


Thank you open source communities, and contributors.

Development
